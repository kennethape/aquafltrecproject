Filtration System

4 Stages of
Pure Water

Our multi-stage system eliminates virtually all water contaminants. Fill from any standard tap — the bottle does the work.

500L
per filter cartridge — easy twist-off replacement
S1
Pre-Filter Mesh
Blocks sediment, rust & large particles from entering the bottle.
S2
Activated Carbon Block
Absorbs chlorine, bad odours, and organic compounds. Improves taste.
S3
Ion Exchange Resin
Removes heavy metals — lead, mercury, copper. Softens water.
S4
Hollow Fibre Membrane
Filters bacteria, cysts & microplastics down to 0.1 micron. Final stage.

12
BEAN
Kinetic Energy System

How Kinetic Charging Works

01
YOU MOVE
Walking, running, shaking — any body movement counts. The internal weight oscillates.
02
ROTOR SPINS
The oscillation spins a micro-generator inside the base — same concept as a kinetic watch.
03
ENERGY STORED
The micro-generator converts mechanical energy into electricity, stored in a small cell.
04
DISPLAY POWERED
Stored energy powers the LED display, filter sensor, temperature chip — no plug needed.

"No battery running low at the wrong time. Your lifestyle charges your bottle."

TAKI
How To Use

6 Simple Steps

01
Fill Up
Open the lid and fill from any tap, stream, or fountain. Works anywhere.
02
Seal & Press
Close the lid firmly. The filter activates automatically as you drink.
03
Check Display
Glance at the cap. Confirm filter life is above 20% before drinking.
04
Drink Clean
Sip through the filtered spout — purified water, instantly.
05
⚡ Move to Charge
Carry the bottle with you. Walking, gym, commuting — motion generates charge.
06
Replace Filter
After ~500L (3–6 months). Twist off the top section, swap in a new cartridge.

Q & A

If anyone has any questions, please feel free to ask — we'll do our best to answer.

Q: HOW LONG DOES THE FILTER LAST?
The filter cartridge lasts approximately 500 litres — around 3 to 6 months of normal daily use.
Q: HOW LONG TO CHARGE BY KINETIC?
Walking for about an hour charges enough for several days of display operation. Vigorous shaking charges faster.
Q: HOW MUCH WILL IT COST?
The product targets mid-range pricing, making it affordable for students and travellers globally.
